BBA PROGRAMME

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a 3 yrs Full time UG programme, affiliated to Vikram University Ujjain. Approved By Higher Education Deptt. MP Govt.

WHY BBA From PIMD :-

The program has evolved to meet the growing demand of the corporate sector by honing the soft skills of the students and preparing them for future assignments. The curriculum is designed in a holistic way by creating synergy between broad based education and development of expertise in area of choice in the main discipline. The program meet the needs of MNCs where the students can seek employment. The teaching methodology involves case and movie based learning for the students to inculcate interest and seeking participation.
