Prestige Institute of Management Dewas (PIMD)


Prestige Education Society in its endeavor to develop highly skilled human resource established the first management Institute in Indore in 1994. The management Institutes of Gwalior and Mandsaur were established in 1997. In the year 2001 with the permission of AICTE, the Institute of Mandsaur was shifted to Dewas at its permanent site for providing better services, coverage and local penetration.
